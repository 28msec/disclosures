SEC Disclosures

Best Practices in SEC Presentation and Disclosure

This app automates the process of obtaining every disclosure made by US public companies to the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). Information is obtained by reading XBRL-based financial SEC filings.

Access approximately 1000 high quality disclosures provided by US public companies; policies, footnote level disclosures (Level 1 information), and specific disclosures (Level 3 information).
